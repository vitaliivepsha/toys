Which credits are refunded to my original payment method?

Refunds for returns are usually issued back to your original payment method.

Unused Exceptional Concepts gift card balances, and refunds to an Exceptional Concepts gift card are also stored in your account.

How long do credits take to apply to my store card/outside payment method?

Posting timeframes for credits to your outside method of payment is dependent on that financial institution’s posting timeframes. On average, it can take up to 5 business days for a credit to become available. If you have questions, we recommend reaching out to your financial institution directly for more information.

How/where/when can I verify that my credit was received?

Refunds issued back to an Exceptional Concepts gift card/ in store credit can be checked in the Payment Preferences & Balances under Account Settings.

If you do not see the credit(s), it is recommended you log out of your account and log back in to allow the balances to refresh.

For refunds back to an original outside payment source (Credit card, debit card, paypal, progressive leasing, etc.), please check with them directly for information on posting timeframes.
